Sunset Cliffs & Tide Pools

‚è∞ 2:45 PM - 5:30 PM
üìç Ladera St & Sunset Cliffs Blvd, Point Loma (5 min from Johnny Ma√±anas!)

Stunning coastal views, dramatic cliff formations, and amazing tide pools! Just a short walk from Johnny Ma√±anas.

üåä Tide Pool Access:
  • Park on Ladera St (free street parking)
  • Look for the steep staircase at corner of Ladera St & Sunset Cliffs Blvd
  • Stairs lead directly down to tide pools
  • Best at low tide - check tide charts! (Look for negative numbers)
  • North of the staircase has more rocky pools with sea life
  • Find hermit crabs, anemones, barnacles, and other creatures!
ü•æ Safety Tips:
  • Wear comfortable, grippy shoes - rocks can be slippery
  • Stay on marked trails and away from cliff edges
  • Bring water and sunscreen
  • Be careful on the steep stairs
  • Great photo opportunities everywhere!
